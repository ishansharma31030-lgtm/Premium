At Datum Sync Inc. ("Datum Sync", "we", "us", or "our"), we understand that architectural and engineering models are highly confidential intellectual property. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website, Autodesk Add-ins, and enterprise synchronization services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and billing details provided during registration.
  • Telemetry Data: Application performance metrics, error logs, and synchronization latency statistics to help us debug issues.
  • Device Information: Hardware specifications, OS version, and Revit version strictly for compatibility and troubleshooting.

2. Handling of Proprietary BIM Data

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Datum Sync acts strictly as a transit layer. We do not permanently store, claim ownership of, or train AI models on your proprietary Revit geometries, floor plans, or project metadata.

When utilizing our cloud synchronization engine, parameter delta updates are encrypted end-to-end. Cached packets are automatically purged from our servers within 24 hours of successful transmission to your central model.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected administrative and telemetry data exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your API keys and manage software licensing.
  • Provide customer support and technical troubleshooting.
  • Monitor server health and optimize synchronization speeds across global nodes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent account access.

4. Security Standards & Compliance

Datum Sync is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure. All data in transit is secured using TLS 1.3 encryption, and data at rest is secured via AES-256.

We undergo annual third-party penetration testing and maintain SOC2 Type II compliance. For enterprise customers utilizing our On-Premise deployment tier, no data leaves your internal corporate firewall.
